Universidad Distrital Francisco Jose de Caldas

The Universidad Distrital Francisco José de Caldas is a public higher education institution located in Bogotá, Colombia. Founded in 1948, the university stands out for its focus on the comprehensive training of professionals, especially in the areas of science, engineering, education, art and technology. As one of the leading universities in the country, it is characterized by its commitment to academic excellence, research and social development, contributing to the well-being and progress of the capital and national community. The university also offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, promoting inclusion and accessibility to higher education.
